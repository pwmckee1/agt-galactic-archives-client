AGT Galactic Archives

"Here it began. Here we became."

Welcome to the AGT Galactic Archives! This is a collaborative catalog for documenting in-game discoveries across the No Man's Sky universe. Submit your findings and help the community build a comprehensive database of galactic regions, star systems, planets, fauna, bases, settlements, and points of interest.

Whether you've found a rare planet, cataloged a new species, or established a base, share your discovery to help fellow Travellers explore with purpose.
